\subsection{Detailed Description}
A thin wrapper around a browfile, or a placeholder which allows users to choose \char`\"{}Other Mac Account\char`\"{} or \char`\"{}Choose File\char`\"{} instead of an existing browfile. 

A browfile choice which wraps a browfile will have a non-nil browfile ivar and a nil selector.~ A browfile choice which wraps a placeholder will have a nil browfile and a non-nil selector.
